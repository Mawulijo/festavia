Festavia

is festival oriented consortium dedicated to preserving the essence and history of festivals worldwide. This has come as a neccesity due to loss and discrepancy of vital information about these memorable seasons. We at Festavia seek to make records of these occasions accessible, transparent and consistent to the masses.

Gist about us...

Excellent Festival Video Coverage

Over the years, FESTAVIA has become the benchmark for video coverage for festivals. We have coverage of over hundreds of festival across the globe. Hence we have a wealth of catalogue making us the the most prefered reference point for historians and researchers.

Sponsored Yearly Trips

Not only do we provide information to you through the internet, we especially take delight in fulfilling our coperate-social responsibilities by bringing celebrations to your doorsteps. This is achieved by way of organising free trips for schools and organisations to help participants witness how some of the most popular festivals are celebrated and why.
